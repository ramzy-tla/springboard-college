About Us

Welcome to Springboard College

Springboard College is a private Senior High and WASSCE Remedial school at Ritz Junction, Madina, Accra. We believe every student deserves quality education and an experienced hand guiding them toward their future — whether that's completing Forms 1 to 3, retaking WASSCE, or preparing for study abroad.

With affordable fees, flexible payment options and a staff that genuinely invests in each learner, we've built a community where achievement is expected and every student is pushed to reach it.
