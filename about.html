Springboard College · Madina, Accra

About Springboard

A private Senior High School built around one idea — prepare every student to clear WASSCE and go further.

Who We Are

Patient, purposeful learning.

Springboard College is a private Senior High School in Madina, Accra, offering Senior High (Forms 1–3), WASSCE remedial support and international test prep. We keep classes structured and caring, so every student gets the attention they need to make real progress.

What drives us

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To be a leading private senior high school in Accra where every student is prepared, supported and inspired to strive for excellence — in the classroom and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide disciplined, caring and high-quality education that helps every student clear WASSCE, discover their strengths and take confident steps toward university and life.

How we teach

Our approach

Experiential Learning

Scheduled lab work, student presentations and practical classroom activity turn ideas into confidence.

Holistic Development

Sports, culture, pastoral care and a dependable weekly structure help students grow as people.

Academic Rigour

Small-class attention, weekly assessment and remedial catch-up make progress visible and purposeful.
