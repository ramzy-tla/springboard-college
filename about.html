Our Story

The best private SHS & WASSCE remedial school in the area

Springboard College sits at Ritz Junction in Madina, Accra, and has built a name as a private Senior High School and WASSCE Remedial institution focused on one thing: quality education that prepares students for what comes next.

We serve students working through the full Senior High curriculum from Form 1 to Form 3, as well as students who need to re-sit WASSCE and get back on track. Alongside that, we run dedicated preparation classes for students planning to study abroad, covering the SAT, IELTS, TOEFL and GRE.

Every part of how we operate — from our experienced teaching staff to our flexible fee structure — is designed around making sure a student's circumstances never stand in the way of their result.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education that prepares every student — whether in Forms 1 to 3, WASSCE remedial classes, or international test prep — for real achievement, not just a certificate.

Our Vision

To be recognized as the leading private Senior High and remedial school in Madina and greater Accra — known for results, discipline and genuine community pride.
