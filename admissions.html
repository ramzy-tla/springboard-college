How It Works

Our admissions process

Enquire

Call, message us on Facebook, or visit the campus at Ritz Junction to ask about the program that fits.

Assessment

New and re-sitting students go through a short placement assessment so we can recommend the right track.

Register

Complete registration and discuss a fee structure and payment plan that works for your family.

Begin Classes

Join classes Monday to Saturday, 7:00 AM – 5:00 PM, with full support from our teaching staff.

Who Can Apply

  • Students entering Form 1, or transferring into Form 2 or 3
  • Students who need to re-sit WASSCE
  • Students preparing for SAT, IELTS, TOEFL or GRE
  • Placement cases needing academic track guidance

Fees & Payment

Springboard College offers affordable fees with flexible payment options, so cost doesn't stand between a student and the right academic support.

Contact our admissions team directly for the current fee schedule for your program of interest.
